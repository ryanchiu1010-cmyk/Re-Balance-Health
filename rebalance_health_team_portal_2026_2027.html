Project Vision

A family can include different generations, different locations, and different health needs. Re-Balance Health helps families collect, understand, assess, and care for their health through one connected AI-powered health journey.

Collect
Interpret
Assess
Care

Problem

Health data is scattered across hospitals, apps, wearable devices, and paper records. Families often struggle to find, understand, and share important health information.

Solution

Re-Balance Health creates a Family Health Memory supported by AI Copilot and smartphone-based Health Assessment.

Impact

The platform supports teen athletes, families, caregivers, and older adults by turning fragmented data into a clear health journey.
