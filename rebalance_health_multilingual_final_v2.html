Impact

Why It Matters

Re-Balance Health helps family members across different age groups turn scattered health data into a clear, trackable health journey while protecting personal privacy.

Through AI medical information interpretation and smartphone-camera-based human balance monitoring, users can identify potential risks earlier, better understand their health status, and move from reactive treatment toward proactive prevention.
